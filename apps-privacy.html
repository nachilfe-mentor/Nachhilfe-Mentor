Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Scope of Application

This Privacy Policy specifically governs the use of the mobile applications "BudgetBlick: Finance Tracker", "Simple Score Keeper & Counter", "AI Photo PDF: Scanner App" and "RealEst Calc: Real Estate ROI" (hereinafter referred to as the "Apps"), developed and published by Thümmler AI GmbH.

This policy describes data processing inside the Apps. Visits to this website may be measured with privacy-preserving, cookieless PostHog analytics as described in the Nachhilfe Mentor privacy policy.

2. No Data Collection

We respect your privacy. All Apps are designed to function entirely offline.

We do not collect, process, or store any personal data.

You do not need to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information (such as name, email, or phone number) to use the Apps.

3. Local Data Storage Only

Any data you enter into the Apps (e.g., financial entries, player names, scores, counters, history, scanned documents, images, PDFs) is stored exclusively locally on your device.

This data is never transmitted to Thümmler AI GmbH servers or any third-party servers. If you uninstall the Apps or clear the App data in your device settings, all locally stored information will be permanently deleted.

4. Third-Party Services & Tracking

To ensure a completely private experience in our utility applications:

  • We do not use any analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics).
  • We do not use any advertising networks or display ads.
  • We do not use user tracking or fingerprinting technologies.
